What is Sitecore Experience Accelerator (SxA)?

Sitecore Experience Accelerator (SxA) is—

  • built on top of Sitecore’s Habitat (a real Sitecore project implemented on the Sitecore Experience Platform to show how Helix is applied and lets developers experience a project based on these principles)
  • adheres to Helix guidelines & recommendations (a set of design principles and conventions for Sitecore development)
  • is completely extensible and upgradable using Sitecore Helix Compliance standards
  • based on Cognifide's Zen Garden
