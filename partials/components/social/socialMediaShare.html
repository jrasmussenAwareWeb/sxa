Social Share

Allowed In

  • Accordion
  • Container
  • Footer
  • Modal Window
  • Row
  • Row Advanced

Usage

Use Social Share to share individual site pages to social media applications — for example providing users the ability to share product informational pages to Facebook or Twitter.

The Social Share component uses a third party service — addThis .
