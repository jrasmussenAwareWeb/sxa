Tips & Troubleshooting

        
Editing Carousel Slides

        
If you have added more than one Carousel Slide and cannot see it in the editor, make sure you have saved.

            Once saved, the editor will scroll through the slides. When the slide you wish to edit is present - click the edit icon.

            Alternatively, you can reorder the slides so that the one you wish to edit is at position 1.
            To reorder carousel slides, click the up or down arrow icons .

        
Image Sizing

        
Size background images to a relative height and width that you wish for display prior to upload.
